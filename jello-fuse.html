About Jello Fuse

Jello Fuse is a free-to-play puzzle game for all ages. There are over 100 unique levels with a little over half being custom made to be more difficult than the next. If you're up for the challenge, download and play now on any iOS or Android device. New update coming in 2023 will introduce all new features including a home screen, level selector, and much more!
