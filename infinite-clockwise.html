About Infnite Clockwise

Infinite Clockwise is a free to play hyper-casual game for all ages. Play by tapping on your screen while collecting points and dodging obstacles. The higher score you get, the harder the game becomes. Compete against friends to see who can get the highest score. Share your score by using our in-game screenshot option or compete against other players using the leaderboards.
